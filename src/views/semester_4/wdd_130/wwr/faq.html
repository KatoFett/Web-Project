Frequently Asked Questions

When is Whitewater Rafting open?

Whitewater Rafting is open from June 1st to August 18th and runs from 6am to 8pm.

Do we have to buy our own equipment beforehand?

No, in fact you don't need to buy any equipment! We offer rentals at a low price for your party.

Is footwear required?

Yes and it must stay on for your safety. We offer rentals if you do not have appropriate footwear.

How long is the adventure?

About 13 minutes.

Is food available?

Yes. There are small restaurants within walking distance which offer a wide variety of food.

Will I have to sign a waiver?

Yes. You will be asked to sign an electronic waiver before paying admission if you have not already.

Are the discounts?

We may offer sales discounts for a limited time. See our special deals page.

What is the refund policy?

We do not offer refunds. In the unlikely event you receive faulty equipment from our rentals, you will be given a replacement.

Can I schedule ahead?

Yes. Contact us to schedule a reservation. Reserving your spot in advance may be cheaper than purchasing tickets up-front.

Can I cancel or move a reservation?

Yes. Contact us to adjust your existing reservation.

My question isn't in the FAQ. Can I contact you?

Yes, feel free to reach out to us!
